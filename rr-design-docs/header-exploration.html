Recommendation
Use Option A — context-sensitive placement
The monogram is your strongest brand asset — it's distinctive, scales to any size, and feels luxury. Inside the app, users already know where they are, so the icon alone is enough and keeps the header clean. On the landing page and auth screens, the full lockup (icon + wordmark) does the job of introducing the brand to new users. Option B is valid but redundant once the mark is recognized. Option C leaves the icon on the shelf entirely.
